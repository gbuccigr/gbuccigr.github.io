So what is this Sample Template Page?

This Sample Template Page is an example of a static web page that can be created for you. Once create the static web page can be placed on a hosting site so that anyone with a web browser can access it. This makes it possible for you to define what you want the world to see on your site.

Many choices available for your web page!

There are many different tabs and themes to choose from. Custom tabs can easily be created and links to other public sites for specific documents, pictures and more can be added. Once the static web page is created and finalized we will provide the entire set of files that make up the site. You can choose to place the files on your hosting site or we can work with you to get it hosted.

Introduce the world to your business!

So show the world what your business has to offer and allow them to easily find you. Simply place your static web page address on your business cards.

What we need from you:

  • Title of your new web page!
  • Picture of your business card.
  • Copy of you logo or artwork if you have any.
  • Tabs to include in your page.
  • Your specific vision for your web page.
